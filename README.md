# Bayer-Pest-Control-Market-Analysis
Analyzed pest control market using product lifecycle theory, the BCG matrix, and text mining in Python

## 1. Project Background
Identify new growth opportunities in Bayer’s pest control market.

## 2. My Role
**Data Analyst**

## 3. Results
- Successfully identified new market opportunities  
- Increased company GMV by **30%**, exceeding expectations

---

## 4. Methodology

### 4.1 Market Potential Analysis
- Overall market trend analysis (Industry Lifecycle Theory)
- Market share distribution, trend changes, and annual growth by segment  
  → Used **BCG Matrix** to classify product categories
- Market concentration analysis  
  → Transaction index used to approximate market share

**Key Findings**
- The pest control market is in a **rapid growth stage**, transitioning toward maturity
- Rodent & insecticide category holds **>60% market share** and ~40% growth → **Star product category**
- Market is not monopolized; competition is fragmented and intense

---

### 4.2 Opportunity Analysis — Rodent & Insecticide Category
- Product sub-category segmentation (identify high-share niches)
- Price segmentation  
  - Compared revenue share, average product revenue, and competition intensity
  - Identified potential price-band opportunities
- Attribute analysis  
  - Store type, model type, physical form vs competition level

**Key Findings**
- Focus categories: **Rodent control** and **Cockroach control**
- Rodent control:
  - Main market: ¥0–50 (high competition)
  - Premium niche: ¥200–250 (~10% share, low competition → opportunity)

**Within ¥0–50 segment**
- ¥10–20: large demand + low competition → strong opportunity
- ¥20–30: also promising
- Tmall stores outperform Taobao
- Sticky mouse boards dominate, but model “0005” shows good share with lower competition
- Solid form products are widely accepted by consumers

---

### 4.3 Competitive Analysis
Top 3 brands analyzed: **Bayer, Keling Pest Control, Ansu**

Analysis dimensions:
- Product category distribution (target customers & markets)
- Product structure — **BCG Matrix**
  - Star: focus on both share & growth
  - Cash Cow: focus on share (rank by revenue)
  - Question Mark: focus on growth (rank by growth rate)
- Traffic channel structure (Top 10 traffic sources)
- Public sentiment analysis (word cloud & keyword extraction)

**Conclusion**
Bayer holds a clear competitive advantage.
